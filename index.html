Frontend Projects

During my internship as a frontend developer, I worked on real-world projects building responsive websites for clients. I also developed several personal and course-related projects that strengthened my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These experiences laid the foundation for my shift toward backend engineering, where I now find my deepest interest.

Always Learning

I'm a firm believer in lifelong learning. I've taken both free and paid online courses from platforms like Pluralsight, ALX, and SheCodes. These programs have helped me explore a wide range of technical skills — from frontend frameworks to backend development tools.

Giving Back

Helping others is a core part of who I am. That’s why I volunteer through the United Nations Online Volunteering platform, using my growing expertise to contribute to meaningful global projects. For me, giving back without expecting anything in return is both fulfilling and motivating.

Beyond Code

When I'm not coding, you’ll find me enjoying three of my favorite things: delicious food, spending time with animals, and writing stories. These passions keep me grounded and creatively inspired.
